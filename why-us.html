Our Promise

8 Reasons to
Choose TruTech

⚡
01
Same-Day Service

Most repairs are done while you wait — in 30 to 60 minutes. We know your phone is your lifeline, so we move fast. No leaving your device overnight. No waiting days for a callback.

⚓
02
Proudly Local

We live here, work here, and care about this community. As a locally owned shop in Cheticamp, we treat every customer like a neighbour — because you are. No corporate scripts, no runaround.

🛡
03
90-Day Warranty

Every repair we perform comes with a full 90-day warranty on both parts and labour. If anything we fixed fails within that window, we'll fix it again — free of charge. No questions asked.

🔬
04
Premium Parts Only

We use OEM-grade and premium aftermarket components exclusively. Cheap parts fail quickly, void warranties, and damage your device. We'd rather charge a fair price for quality than cut corners.

💰
05
Free Diagnostics

We examine your device and tell you exactly what's wrong and what it costs — before we do any work. You decide. No pressure. If you choose not to proceed, the diagnostic is completely free.

🔒
06
Your Data is Safe

We have a strict policy: we never access your personal data, apps, photos, or messages. Your device is repaired, not browsed. Privacy is a right, and we take it seriously.

📞
07
Real People, Real Support

Call us, walk in, or message us — you'll speak with a real technician, not a bot. We explain what happened to your device in plain language so you understand exactly what we're doing and why.

✅
08
No Fix, No Charge

On most repairs, if we can't fix it, you don't pay. We'll tell you upfront if we think a repair might not be possible — before we start. Transparent, honest, and fair. Every time.
